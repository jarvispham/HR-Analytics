# 📊 HR Workforce & Diversity Analytics Dashboard

## 🎯 Project Overview
This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of a corporate workforce, focusing on **Employee Retention**, **Diversity & Inclusion**, and **Succession Planning**. 

The goal was to transform raw HR data into a strategic tool that helps leadership understand why employees leave and how to build a more balanced, future-proof workforce.

---

## 🚀 Key Features & Technical Implementation

* **Dynamic Storytelling Tabs:** Used **Bookmark Navigators** to create an "Analysis Page" with separate views for **Business Problems** and **Key Findings**, allowing for a clean, narrative-driven user experience.
* **Advanced Multi-Category Search:** Implemented **Field Parameters** to create a single, dynamic search bar that allows users to toggle between searching by Name, Department, Job Title, or State.
* **Master Filter Summary:** Developed a complex **DAX measure** that displays a real-time "breadcrumb" trail of all active filters at the top of the dashboard.
* **Automated Data Cleaning:** Utilized **Power Query** to calculate employee age, tenure, and employment status (Active vs. Terminated) from raw hire and termination dates.

---

## 💡 Strategic Insights (The "So What?")

### 1. Retention & Turnover
* **Finding:** The organization has a **12.43% turnover rate**, with a significant concentration of departures in the **Engineering** and **Finance** departments.
* **Action:** Recommended targeted retention interviews and compensation reviews for technical hubs.

### 2. The Generational Gap
* **Finding:** Over **95%** of the workforce is over the age of 30, with **Gen Z representing less than 5%** of the total headcount.
* **Action:** Highlighted a critical need for junior talent recruitment to prevent institutional knowledge loss as senior staff approach retirement.

### 3. Diversity & Equality
* **Finding:** The company maintains an excellent **50/50 gender split** and a robust ethnic distribution (**29% White, 16% Black, 16% Asian, 11% Latino**).
* **Action:** Leveraged this as a key competitive advantage for employer branding and recruitment.
